SECURITY IMPROVEMENTS
================================================================================

BEFORE:
  Config: threshold=1, admins=[Monitor, SuperAdmin]
  Action: Monitor calls pause()
  Result: ✗ FAILS - threshold met, role check PASSES, pause executes

AFTER:
  Config: threshold=1, admins=[Monitor, SuperAdmin]
  Action: Monitor calls pause()
  Result: ✅ BLOCKED - threshold met, role check FAILS, pause blocked

MECHANISM:
  Success = (Multisig Threshold Met) AND (All Signers Have Permission)
